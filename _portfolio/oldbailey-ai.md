Developed an AI to assist researchers and historians by providing insights into historical trends, facilitating data analysis, and offering an immersive experience through role-play. Architected a LLM persona chat (judge/defendant/historian) trained over 2,500+ records, enabling natural language interrogation and richer, seminar-grade insights.

**Tech Stack:** Next.js, Flask, TypeScript, OpenAI, Python, MongoDB
